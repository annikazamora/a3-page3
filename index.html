Breakfast Foods

Arguably the most important meal of the day

Mel's Diner - 140 University Ave W, Waterloo, ON

BYO Omelette: Spinach, Ham. Served with homefries
Price: $11.49 CAD (Not Including Tax)

Eggs were nicely done and did not give me the egg ick. The homefries were quite tasty however needed a bit more salt. Not sure if the bread was necessary but I love bread so I'll let that one slide. Very large portion just like majority of their other dishes. Not bad at all but could be better which is why it's not a 10/10.

Overall Rating: 7.5/10
Would I Repurchase?: Maybe

Cafe Landwer - 3401 Dufferin St, North York, ON

Eggs Benedict Florentine
Price: $14.95 CAD (Not Including Tax)

Hollandaise sauce carried the whole dish, it was so tasty. The eggs were cooked just right. The potatoes were good, but very largely cut which made it feel heavy. Felt very full walking around the mall after this.

Overall Rating: 9.5/10
Would I Repurchase?: Absolutely and I would get two
