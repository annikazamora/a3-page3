My Aunt's House - Location is not necessary

Mini Chocolate Cupcake
Price: Free

One of the best parts of family gatherings (other than family of course) is the food. My cousin is a great baker and I look forwards to whatever she decides to bake because I already know I will be eating it all up. Not a fan of sprinkles but since there are a countable number of them it's okay. Chocolate is also the best flavour.

Overall Rating: 10/10
Would I Repurchase?: Not applicable since it was free, but I would 100% eat again

State & Main - 578 Weber St, Waterloo, ON, Canada

Pretzel Bites
Price: $17 CAD + tax

Not typically a huge fan of pretzels because they're always so dry but these weren't dry. I liked the cheese dip, however I'm not sure who decided to put the whole bag of salt on every pretzel stick. Felt quite thristy after every pretzel.

Overall Rating: 5.8/10
Would I Repurchase?: Unfortunately no

Rogers Centre - 1 Blue Jays Way, Toronto, ON, Canada

Loonie Dog
Price: $1 CAD + tax

**Only available on Loonie Dog nights (non-Loonie Dog nights: $4.65 + tax)**

Never had the urge to watch baseball but I definitely had the urge to get a Loonie Dog, or what I like to call it, a dollar dog. It is quite small but since it's only $1 at the Rogers Centre, this is considered a huge deal. Tried it with mustard where I realized that mustard maybe isn't so bad. This is in the "snack" category because I could eat it in 2 bites.

Overall Rating: 9/10
Would I Repurchase?: For a dollar: yes, regular price: no

Starbucks - 550 King St N, Waterloo, ON, Canada

Racoon Cake Pop
Price: $3.45 CAD + tax

This is the best limited edition cake pop design starbucks has created. The cake was vanilla inside, and it literally was so prefect I repurchased another one a few days later. Such a good sweet treat, so sad it's seasonal and not here all year round, but that's what makes it special.

Overall Rating: 1000000/10
Would I Repurchase?: Yes

Krispy Kreme Doughnuts - 5920 Mavis Rd, Mississauga, ON, Canada

Assorted 12 Pack: Cinnamon Apple Filled, Chocolate Iced Kreme Filled, Cake Batter, Oreo Cookies and Kreme, Strawberry Iced with Sprinkles, Caramel Kreme Crunch
Price: $15.50 CAD + tax

Honestly did not try all flavours of the donuts because I would actually throw up. Not a big fan of donuts to begin with but I will say the strawberry iced and cake batter are definitely the better ones in this assortment. Definitely not a bad choice for a sweet treat but honestly I like to keep it simple and would much rather have the original glazed donut. You can't beat the classic one.

Overall Rating: 6/10
Would I Repurchase?: Probably not since there are better options
